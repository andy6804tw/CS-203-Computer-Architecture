Target Audience

  • This is a required course for all degree-seeking undergraduate students in EE and CSE.
  • An understanding of the material in this course is strongly recommended for all students who intend to take ECE 568/668 in Computer Architecture.

Overall goals of ECE 232

  • Acquire the ability to use a hierarchical (layered) approach to understand a complex system
    • Hardware
    • Software
    • This ability is useful in many EE courses and on the job
  • Acquire skills to program in assembly language
  • Understand the principals of hardware software interface
  • Learn the principles of datapath and control design for scalar, pipelined computer systems
  • Understand memory hierarchy and the principles of virtual memory
  • Become aware of current design issues and concerns
