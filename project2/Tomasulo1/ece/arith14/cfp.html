ARITH-14
14th IEEE Symposium on Computer Arithmetic

April 14-16, 1999
Adelaide, Australia

CALL FOR PAPERS

Authors are invited to submit papers describing recent advances on all aspects of computer arithmetic, including, but not restricted to the following topics:
  • Foundations of number systems and arithmetic;
  • Arithmetic processor design and implementation;
  • Arithmetic algorithms and their analysis;
  • Highly parallel arithmetic units and systems;
  • New floating-point units and systems;
  • Standards for number representation and arithmetic;
  • Impact of high level languages on arithmetic systems;
  • Special function implementation; and
  • Applications of Computer Arithmetic for cryptography, DSP, data compression and alike.

Postscript version of the complete paper, and a separate text file with title and abstract, should be submitted by ftp no later than:

September 1, 1998

following the instructions in the conference web site:

/ece/arith14/

Further instructions about submission and information about the symposium will be posted at the web site. Submissions should conform to the maximum length requirement for the proceedings of the conference, as stated below, but for reviewing should preferably be formatted in normal article style (about 20 pages, single column, single spacing). Authors should include an e-mail contact address, since all correspondence will be done by e-mail. Acceptance will be notified in December 1998, and final camera-ready papers (up to 8 pages, two columns) will be due in January 1999. Conference proceedings will be available at the Symposium.
