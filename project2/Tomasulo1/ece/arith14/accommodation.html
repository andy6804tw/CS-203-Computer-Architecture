ARITH 14

14th IEEE Symposium on Computer Arithmetic

Adelaide, South Australia

April 14 - 16, 1999

Where to Stay...

The Conference will be held at the Hotel Adelaide International, located in North Adelaide, just a short stroll from the city centre and close to the shops and cosmopolitan cafes of O'Connell Street. With its spectacular elevated views of the city and glorious surrounding gardens, the Hotel enjoys a prime location in Adelaide. The hotel has 140 rooms and suites, most of which offer spectacular views of the city skyline.

Offering excellent accommodation at affordable prices, (Special symposium rate of AU$80 incl. tax & service per night, single/double. This rate is guaranteed through March 26), the Hotel provides all facilities and amenities to make your stay as comfortable as possible. A booking form is HERE for the Hotel Adelaide.

The Hotel offers Executive suites with luxury spas and two roomed suites with spas at A$160 incl. tax and service per night.

Budget accommodation in a nearby student hostel, Lincoln College is also available at AU$32 per night (incl. breakfast).

For further details, contact the Local Arrangements Chair,
