Goals

Using the tool, after reading the paper will allow the user to obtain a holistic picture. Here the conventional cache is considered. The values and the graphs provided help the user understand the following important points related to cache energy dissipation (for a given set of cache attributes)

1) What are the various factors involved in cache energy dissipation
2) The ratios among them
3) Effect of variation and comparison of Energy dissipation for 4 different values of Supply voltages, associativity, cache size and line size.
